Born in 1988, China. Working and living in the Gadigal Country, sovereignty was never ceded.

Diane Dandan Feng is an independent curator whose curatorial practice revolves around hybrid identities, algorithmically shaped social actions, digital activism and cyber culture. Feng had worked years in the technology industry and cofounded a EdTech startup before she trained as a curator at the University of the Arts London.
